# CRM Lead and Follow-up Playbook

**Timestamp:** 15:34, 24.07.2026  
**Status:** ACTIVE OPERATING PLAYBOOK

## Lead intake

Capture full name, business email, company, country, inquiry type, problem/application/outcome, project stage and source. Recommended fields include role, website, product interest, substrate, environment, acceptance criteria, scale, current solution, target date, budget status, decision role, required documents and preferred channel.

Public intake must not request credentials, bank data, sensitive personal information or uncontrolled confidential uploads. Submission does not create a quote, warranty, availability promise or contract.

## Validation and conversion

1. Verify email/domain and organization where reasonably possible.
2. Search Accounts, Contacts, Leads and Opportunities for duplicates.
3. Separate confirmed facts from self-reported/unverified claims.
4. Identify confidentiality, regulatory or source risks.
5. Score, prioritize and assign an owner.
6. Define next action, due date and source reference.
7. Convert only when Account/Contact links, product/application, problem/outcome, stage, case status and owner are sufficiently clear.
8. Preserve the original Lead and record created IDs.

## Approved case statuses

- `ACTION_REQUIRED_NTSN`
- `WAITING_EXTERNAL`
- `WAITING_INTERNAL`
- `SCHEDULED`
- `DORMANT`
- `CLOSED_WON`
- `CLOSED_LOST`
- `CLOSED_NO_ACTION`

## Follow-up timing defaults

Use explicit commitments first. Otherwise:

- urgent deadline/production block/payment decision: same or next business day;
- quote/proposal/technical package: 2–3 business days;
- waiting for customer/supplier/partner: 5 business days;
- meeting/sample/test/pilot completed: 2 business days;
- stated leave/review period: first suitable business day after return/period;
- dormant strategic lead: 14–30 days;
- long-term milestone: 3–5 business days before milestone.

## Internal Calendar reminder

Search Calendar first. Use `Follow up — [Company/contact] / [case]`, 15 minutes, no attendees, private, transparent, no video meeting, popup 30 minutes before, Europe/Oslo. Description: case summary; latest confirmed status; required action; dependency/risk; source note.

## Execution at reminder time

Re-read the latest thread and sources; check whether the dependency arrived; close/reschedule if resolved; otherwise prepare the useful draft/review/decision request; update the register; create another reminder only for a genuine remaining action. Never send or create external commitments without explicit approval.
