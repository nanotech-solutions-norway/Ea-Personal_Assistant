# CRM Master Instructions

**Timestamp:** 15:34, 24.07.2026  
**Status:** CONTROLLED WORKING STANDARD

## Source authority

Apply sources in this order:

1. Current explicit user instruction.
2. Latest `APPROVED` or `CANONICAL` operating rule.
3. Approved CRM record/register.
4. Verified Gmail, Calendar, Drive, GitHub or accounting evidence.
5. Reasonable inference clearly labelled as inference.
6. Unknown or conflicting information classified `PENDING_REVIEW`.

Never silently merge contradictory sources.

## Required active-case controls

Every active Lead, Opportunity or Follow-up must include:

- immutable identifier;
- Account/Contact relationship where known;
- canonical product/application terminology;
- latest substantive contact date;
- current case status and priority;
- confirmed facts separated from inference and unknowns;
- exact next action, owner and due date;
- dependency/risk;
- source reference;
- reminder and closure state.

## Approval boundary

Explicit approval is required before sending/forwarding external email or attachments, creating or materially changing an external invitation, sharing files or permissions, making pricing/delivery/warranty/exclusivity/liability/legal/regulatory commitments, or disclosing one party’s confidential information to another.

Internal drafts, private reminders and controlled registers do not create external commitments.

## Accounting boundary

CRM commercial and accounting tabs are pre-posting, handoff and reconciliation controls. Official invoices, credit notes, VAT, payment and ledger status remain authoritative only in the approved accounting system.

## Confidentiality

Do not store credentials, secrets, bank details, unnecessary personal data or confidential attachments in general CRM records. Link to controlled sources and apply least privilege.

## Quality gate

A record passes only when current context has been reviewed, facts/unknowns are separated, the status is current, a specific action/owner/date exists or the case is formally dormant/closed, duplicate reminders were avoided, source evidence exists and no external commitment bypassed approval.

## Change control

Material schema, workflow, status, privacy, automation or accounting changes require validation evidence and approval classification. Non-substantive maintenance already authorized by canonical rules may be `AUTO_APPROVED`; new policy, unresolved conflict or material risk is `PENDING_REVIEW`.
