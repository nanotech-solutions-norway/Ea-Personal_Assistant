# 09 — Ea Level 2 Managed Backend

Status: HOLD  
Authority: Future design only  
Activation condition: Level 1 finalized, validated and approved

## Hold rule
Level 2 managed automation is not active and must not be implemented until Level 1 Ea is finalized, validated and approved.

Ea must not claim Level 2 capabilities as native Level 1 functions.

## Purpose
Level 2 is the future managed backend layer for capabilities that native Custom GPT + Project + apps cannot reliably provide.

## Level 2 candidate capabilities
- Universal local microphone/speaker capture.
- Automatic live transcription feed.
- Written meeting advice side panel.
- Automatic post-meeting processing.
- Automatic 03:00 file-aware updates.
- Gmail/Calendar/Drive write-back.
- Readback validation.
- Attachment storage and cataloging automation.
- ZIP/RAR extraction automation.
- Action queue with audit trail.
- Backend permission and retention controls.

## Level 2 is required for
- unattended file-aware nightly updates;
- universal live meeting capture across platforms;
- automatic transcript streaming;
- automatic post-meeting write-back;
- guaranteed Drive/GitHub updates with validation;
- archive extraction/storage automation at scale.

## Level 2 is not required for Level 1
Level 1 can still do:

- manual email review;
- manual calendar support;
- meeting memo generation;
- due diligence;
- legal/financial business support;
- manual transcript summary;
- written live advice from user-supplied notes/transcript chunks;
- document generation;
- project learning packs;
- approval-controlled drafts.

## Future requirements to collect after Level 1 validation
- Preferred automation platform: MCP, Google Apps Script, n8n, Make, Zapier, Power Automate or custom API.
- Device/OS for local audio capture.
- Transcription provider requirements.
- Gmail/Calendar/Drive scopes.
- Storage locations.
- Data retention policy.
- Consent logging workflow.
- Audit and readback validation rules.
- Security model.

## Level 2 activation gate
Level 2 may move from HOLD to PLANNING only when:

- Level 1 active-source files are complete;
- Level 1 Project setup is complete;
- Level 1 Custom GPT is configured;
- Level 1 QA validation passes;
- user explicitly approves Level 2 planning;
- Level 2 risks and permissions are documented.
